About us  
ILEYS Consult (ILEYS) is a research; training and consulting company specialized in offering up- to date Practical Solutions on organizational and social development challenges. Registered in South West State Somalia on 2015 and with working experiences in the Somali context especially areas under SWS jurisdiction. ILEYS strives towards innovating sustainable solutions and developing competences within the public, private, and the non-profit sectors. We do this through research, innovation, training, information dissemination and quality technical advice.  
  
ILEYS’ adopts a collaborative approach to research issues, working in partnership with client organizations to improve knowledge and competence in using research findings for decision making. ILEYS is a learning organization, emphasizing teamwork and the sharing of knowledge and expertise in the continuous pursuit of excellence. ILEYS’ first responsibility is to the client. The success of ILEYS is measured by the way we meet our responsibility to our client and the people with whom we do business with. Giving independent and professional feedback is a major part of that responsibility.  
  
ILEYS research team is composed of individuals with a strong background in research spanning over 10 years’ experience and with educational research background in agriculture, food security and livelihoods, natural resource management, Agro-ecology, gender mainstreaming, social protection, WASH, nutrition, economics, water, sanitation and hygiene (WASH), among other sectors. We adopt a collaborative and participatory approach to research issues, working in partnership with client organizations to help them make accurate, insightful and timely decisions.  
  
Some of our social research services include, baseline, mid-term & end-line evaluations,Labour and market assessment, impact evaluations, outcome harvesting, Third Party Monitoring among others.  
  
At ILEYS, we recognize that change is the most constant element in today’s globalized and highly dynamic world. To achieve this, ILEYS brings together a network of experienced professionals who have over years of hands-on experiences in the field of applied research, development, strategic management and institutional development and innovative to develop efficient, effective and practical solutions to everyday challenges at home, work and in our communities.
